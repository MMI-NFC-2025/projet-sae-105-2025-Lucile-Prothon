Movies

Autism Spectrum Disorders, 2023 - ARTE

The ARTE documentary Psycho – Moi, autiste, directed by Antje Behr in 2022, offers an intimate and unfiltered exploration of Autism Spectrum Disorders (ASD). Through the testimony of Charlotte, a French PhD student, and contributions from caregivers, the film highlights the sensory, emotional, and social challenges faced by autistic people.

Astrid et Raphaëlle, 2020 - France TV

Astrid et Raphaëlle follows the investigations of an unusual duo in the Paris criminal police. Astrid Nielsen, a brilliant archivist with Asperger’s syndrome, has an exceptional memory and outstanding analytical skills. Raphaëlle Coste, a pragmatic and empathetic police commander, excels at decoding human behaviour and building connections with witnesses and suspects. Together, they solve complex cases by combining Astrid’s relentless logic with Raphaëlle’s sharp intuition. The series not only explores captivating police investigations but also human dynamics, differences in perception, and the social difficulties Astrid faces.

Temple Grandin, 2010 - HBO Max, Amazon Prime Video

This film traces the real life of Temple Grandin, an autistic woman who became a professor of animal science and a prominent advocate for autistic rights. It shows her journey, her struggles to communicate in a neurotypical world, and how she revolutionised practices in slaughterhouses thanks to her empathy for animals. It is a deeply inspiring work that portrays difference as a strength.

Mozart and the Whale, 2005 - Amazon Prime Video, YouTube Movies

Inspired by a true story, this film follows the love story between Donald and Isabelle, two adults with Asperger’s Syndrome. It explores their hypersensitivity, misunderstandings, and sincere affection with humor and emotion, offering a rare, human view of adult autism.
