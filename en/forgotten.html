The Forgotten
Women of Science

Rehabilitation of Female Figures

Through various books, documentaries, and cultural initiatives, it shows how society is finally giving these pioneers their rightful place, symbolising a fight for visibility and equality in the world of research.

Women in Science in Literature

It highlights how authors have helped bring these long-overlooked figures to light, blending fiction and reality to give a voice back to those whose discoveries were minimised or erased.

Matilda Timeline

It presents a chronology of great female researchers who were unjustly forgotten and parallels their journeys with the social and feminist advances of their time.
